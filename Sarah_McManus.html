Family lore claims Sarah McManus was trained as a  midwife and governess by Mary ‘Puxty’, Governor Bligh’s daughter.

Mary Putland was Governor Bligh’s daughter. Mary, acted as the colony’s first lady from August 1806 to January 1808. She was the wife of John Putland, a naval officer and Bligh’s aide de camp. He died young and before they had children. Mary’s  second marriage was to Macquarie’s deputy, Colonel Maurice O’Connell.  They left Australia in 1814 but returned to the colony in 1834. Maurice having been appointed to command the forces in N.S.W.

Sarah would have been trained after their return by then she would have been 17 years of age.
