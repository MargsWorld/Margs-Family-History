Photos of John Stait’s war experience

 
 

The men of the 2/3rd Independent Company fought in the most difficult conditions and endured enormous hardship while fighting the Japanese. They went largely unrecognised for their efforts. They had all volunteered for the Commandos and were specially selected. All of the members decorated in the 2/3 have said that every member of the unit should have been decorated.Their support of each other lasted a lifetime. During the New Guinea Campaign they had lost 58 killed, 119 Wounded in Action and 226 evacuated sick. They had killed over 969 Japanese in close combat and wounded countless more.
