I began researching family in 1997 when I came across someone looking for information on the name STAIT and saying that two of the Stait family had been convicts. This was my maiden name and this snippet of information triggered my curiosity and my desire to find the truth of my roots.

The journey has taken me across the sea to Wales, Scotland and Ireland and many strange and amazing synchronicities. Dare I say that I believe that I have often had someone tap me on the shoulder and point me in the right direction so that I would know where I came from and all of my family. It has been a privilege to be the keeper and gatherer of history for this wonderful tribe.

I was lucky that the journey began before my parents died and they were able to come along for the ride. Sometimes they were shocked by their forebears, sometimes delighted and sometimes unbelieving.

I am also fortunate to be married to a fey welshman who has often been able to bring through information which has painted the picture for us or sent us in new directions. This website will tell you where this has led us.
